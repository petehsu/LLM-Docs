## GLM-Z1-FlashX

GLM-Z1-FlashX is an enhanced version of the free inference model GLM-Z1-Flash. It features ultra-fast inference speed, higher concurrency guarantees, and exceptional cost-effectiveness, further lowering the usage threshold for inference models.

| **Price** | **Input Modality** | **Output Modality** | **Context Window** | **Maximum Output Tokens** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0.1 RMB per million tokens | Text | Text | 128K | 32K |

### **Recommended Use Cases**

1. **Data Reasoning:** Skilled at handling complex problems such as mathematical proofs, computational logic, and reasoning tasks. Capable of breaking down and deeply analyzing ambiguous instructions, successfully resisting interference and producing the correct final answer.
2. **Job Matching:** Efficiently parses large volumes of resume texts and job descriptions (JDs), accurately extracting key information such as skill tags and experience requirements for intelligent matching. Supports high-frequency resume screening and job recommendations in enterprise HR systems.
3. **Script Generation:** Quickly generates scripts for films, long-form dramas, or short videos based on the subject matter, character settings, and plot frameworks, with real-time interactive adjustments to plot direction and dialogue details.
4. **Professional Report Writing:** Automatically integrates multi-source information such as industry data, policy documents, and academic literature to produce well-structured research reports, feasibility analyses, or market research reports, supporting batch report generation tasks.

### **Resources**

[API Documentation](https://bigmodel.cn/dev/api/Reasoning-models/glm-z1) : Learn how to call the API.

### **Detailed Description**

GLM-Z1-FlashX, as a lightweight solution, retains the full technology stack of GLM-Z1-Air while being more lightweight, faster, and more affordable. Despite having fewer parameters, GLM-Z1-FlashX still excels in complex tasks such as mathematical reasoning, long-document processing, and code generation, with overall performance ranking among the best open-source models of its size. GLM-Z1-FlashX offers developers low-barrier AI experimentation and lightweight deployment support, balancing inference efficiency with inference cost, and is particularly well-suited for high-frequency use cases.
