## GLM-Z1-Air

GLM-Z1-Air is a reasoning model with deep thinking capabilities. It has significantly enhanced mathematical reasoning performance thanks to reasoning data augmentation and alignment optimization, and its cost is only 1/30 that of DeepSeek-R1, making it ideal for high-frequency usage scenarios.

| **Price** | **Input Modality** | **Output Modality** | **Context Window** | **Maximum Output Tokens** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0.5 RMB per million tokens | Text | Text | 128K | 32K |

### **Recommended Use Cases**

1. **Job Matching:** Quickly and accurately extracts skills and job requirements from resumes, generating quantitative match reports based on expertise, skills, experience, etc., providing scientific support for enterprise recruitment.
2. **Script Writing:** Generates complete script content, covering character dialogues, scene transitions, plot conflicts, etc., supporting multi-episode story structures and emotional continuity. (Recommended to combine with [search tools](https://www.bigmodel.cn/dev/howuse/websearch)  to gather trending topics, popular trends, and social interests, ensuring that the script stays current and relevant.)
3. **Literature Comparison Analysis:** Automatically parses the core points and data of large volumes of literature, accurately identifies differences in research methods and related conclusions across studies, and outputs structured comparative analyses and reports.
4. **Low-Resource Language Data Synthesis:** Allows users to use small amounts of high-quality seed data in low-resource languages to synthesize large volumes of similar data without excessive similarity or mixing in other information.

### **Detailed Description**

Built on GLM-4-Air-250414, GLM-Z1-Air uses cold start and expanded reinforcement learning strategies, with deep optimization training on tasks such as math, code, and logic. Compared to the base model, it shows significantly enhanced mathematical reasoning and complex problem-solving abilities. Additionally, it integrates general reinforcement learning techniques based on battle ranking feedback during training, effectively improving the model‚Äôs general capabilities.

In some tasks, GLM-Z1-Air demonstrated strong mathematical reasoning abilities through evaluations on benchmarks like AIME 24/25, LiveCodeBench, and GPQA, enabling it to tackle a wider range of complex tasks.
