## GLM-4-Voice

GLM-4-Voice is Zhipu‚Äôs first end-to-end speech model. It can directly understand and generate Chinese and English speech, enabling real-time voice conversations. It also allows users to flexibly adjust features such as emotion, tone, speed, and dialect of the voice according to their instructions, making voice interactions more natural and vivid.

| **Price** | **Input Modality** | **Output Modality** | **Context Window** | **Maximum Output Tokens** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 80 RMB per million tokens | Audio/Text | Audio | 8K | 4K |

### **Recommended Use Cases**

* **Character Companionship:** The AI engages in emotionally driven conversations with users through virtual characters (such as game characters or virtual idols). These characters can be customized with specific personalities, backgrounds, and voices, providing round-the-clock companionship.
* **Intelligent Tour Guide:** The AI tour guide interacts with users through real-time voice conversations, offering detailed information about historical background, cultural significance, and architectural features. It helps users plan their tour routes through voice descriptions and answers users‚Äô questions about attractions.
* **English Learning:** The AI English teacher engages users in conversation practice by simulating real-life scenarios (such as ordering food or asking for directions). It answers questions about grammar rules, provides real-time pronunciation corrections, teaches daily expressions and grammar knowledge, and offers improvement suggestions.
* **Online Education:** The AI tutor explains course content to students in detail, providing course instruction, homework assistance, and study recommendations across various subjects (such as math, history, and science). It answers students‚Äô homework questions and helps them understand challenging concepts through multi-turn dialogues.

* [API Documentation](https://bigmodel.cn/dev/api/rtav/GLM-4-Voice) : Learn how to call the API.

### **Detailed Description**

With its real-time voice conversation capability, GLM-4-Voice provides users with an efficient and smooth communication experience. GLM-4-Voice supports emotional expression, dialect generation, and adjustable speaking speed, as well as bilingual support for both Chinese and English. Its application scenarios are diverse, covering areas such as virtual character interaction, intelligent education, smart tourism, and child companionship. With flexible voice input and output capabilities, GLM-4-Voice delivers highly efficient and personalized service experiences.

In enterprise applications, GLM-4-Voice can be tailored with specialized solutions for different vertical industries, helping developers quickly adapt to and integrate into the era of large models at a lower cost.
