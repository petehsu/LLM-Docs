## GLM-4-Long

GLM-4-Long is a language model specifically designed for processing ultra-long texts and memory-intensive tasks, supporting inputs up to 1 million tokens (1.5 to 2 million characters).

| **Price** | **Input Modality** | **Output Modality** | **Context Window** | **Maximum Output Tokens** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 RMB per million tokens | Text | Text | 1M | 4K |

### **Recommended Use Cases**

1. **Financial Report Analysis:** Capable of processing hundreds of pages of annual financial reports, automatically extracting key information such as revenue structure, risk warnings, and management discussion, and generating structured analytical reports. By remembering historical financial data, it can compare trends across multiple reports and identify potential financial anomalies.
2. **Academic Paper Reading:** Supports parsing academic papers with tens of thousands of words, automatically summarizing key sections such as research background, experimental methods, and conclusions, and outputs content by topic. Combined with domain knowledge bases, it can logically verify cross-section theoretical derivations, helping researchers quickly identify innovations and argumentative flaws.
3. **Novel Reading:** Performs in-depth analysis of character relationships and plotlines in million-word-length novels, generating specific content summaries. Based on memory of the overall writing style, it enables high-quality continuations that maintain narrative coherence and literary quality.
4. **Legal Document Analysis:** Processes vast amounts of legal provisions, case law, and contract texts, precisely extracting key information such as rights and obligations clauses and definitions of responsibilities. By remembering similar cases, it assists lawyers in conducting legal risk assessments and developing litigation strategies.

### **Detailed Description**

1. **Ultra-Long Text Processing Capability:** Supports context lengths of up to 1M tokens (approximately 1.5 to 2 million characters), equivalent to 2 copies of *Dream of the Red Chamber* or 125 academic papers, demonstrating extremely powerful long-text processing capabilities.
2. **Reasoning Ability:** Not only can it read and translate long documents, but it can also perform global analysis, such as analyzing financial reports and extracting key information, contract clauses, and scientific research data. GLM-4-Long already possesses certain reasoning and memory capabilities, enabling it to understand and respond to complex queries and to create chatbots with ultra-long memory.
3. **Key Technical Points:** During the Continue Pre-Training, Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT), and Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) stages, mixed training was performed to gradually extend the context length, developing from an initial 2K context support to 1M context support.

### **Features Supported**

* Streaming Output
* Structured Output
* Function Calling
