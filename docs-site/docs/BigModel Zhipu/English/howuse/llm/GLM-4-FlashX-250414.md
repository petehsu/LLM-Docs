## GLM-4-FlashX-250414

GLM-4-FlashX-250414 is an enhanced version of the free language model GLM-4-Flash. It features ultra-fast inference speed, higher concurrency guarantees, and exceptional cost-effectiveness, delivering outstanding performance in real-time web search, long-context processing, and multilingual support.

| **Price** | **Input Modality** | **Output Modality** | **Context Window** | **Maximum Output Tokens** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0.1 RMB per million tokens | Text | Text | 128K | 16K |

### **Recommended Use Cases**

1. **Real-Time Customer Service and Multi-Turn Dialogue:** Capable of handling thousands of simultaneous user inquiries while maintaining millisecond-level response times in multilingual interactions. With its 128K ultra-long context understanding capability, it enables coherent and accurate multi-turn question-answering, making it particularly suitable for high-concurrency 24/7 services in scenarios such as cross-border e-commerce and financial services. (Recommended to combine with [search tools](https://www.bigmodel.cn/dev/howuse/websearch) for more real-time, accurate, and up-to-date answers.)
2. **High-Frequency Financial Trading Analysis:** Rapid real-time parsing of market data, generating trading strategy evaluation reports within milliseconds. Supports quantitative trading platforms in completing risk alerts and decision optimizations under high-volatility market conditions. (Recommended to combine with [search tools](https://www.bigmodel.cn/dev/howuse/websearch)  for more real-time, accurate, and up-to-date answers.)
3. **Meeting Summary Generation:** Accurately captures key topics, speaker logic, and decision conclusions based on 128K ultra-long context, quickly generating structured meeting summaries, including agenda reviews, action items, and risk point reminders.
4. **Multilingual Translation and Cross-Cultural Collaboration:** Simultaneously handles translation needs between major languages such as English, Chinese, and Spanish, as well as niche languages. Supports low-latency, high-quality language conversion for multinational companies in cross-border meetings and document collaboration, especially suitable for fields such as law and healthcare that demand extremely high translation accuracy.

* [API Documentation](https://bigmodel.cn/dev/api/normal-model/glm-4) : Learn how to call the API.

### **Detailed Description**

* **Ultra-Long Context:** The model supports a 128K context, equivalent to processing the length of 300 pages of text in a single prompt. This capability allows GLM-4-Flash-250414 to better understand and handle long text content, making it suitable for scenarios that require in-depth context analysis.
* **Ultra-Fast Inference Speed:** The model can complete complex logical processing within milliseconds, ensuring a smooth ‚Äúinstant response‚Äù experience whether it‚Äôs real-time multi-turn dialogue or rapid parsing of massive text data.
* **Multilingual Support:** GLM-4-Flash-250414 has powerful multilingual capabilities, supporting up to 26 languages. This provides global users with multilingual interaction services and broadens the model‚Äôs application scope.
* **Web Search:** Supports external tool calls to retrieve information through online searches, enhancing the quality and timeliness of the language model‚Äôs outputs.
